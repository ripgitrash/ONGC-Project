Internship procedure

  1. Duration

    The duration of summer /winter training will be 1-2 months and for MCA students will be for 6 months.

    Summer/Winetr trainings for college students are provided by ONGC in the following Disciplines.

    2. Engineering
    Electrical Engineering
    Chem. Engineering
    BE/B Tech- Computer Science
    BE/B Tech - IT
    BE/B Tech - Electronics & Telecommunication
    Petroleum Engineering
    Mech. Engineering
    Civil Engineering
  2. Eligibility:

    All regular students undergoing the following courses in Universities/Deemed Universities/Institutions recognised by the University Grant Commission(UGC) and/or approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) shall be eligible to apply for undertaking Students training at ONGC.

    1. Graduate Degree/Post Graduate Degree in above mentioned branches of Engineering.
    2. Graduate Degree/Post Graduate Degree in above mentioned branches in Computer Programming/ Computer Application.

  3. How and When to apply:

    Students will apply on the standard application form after registering their username and passwords on the above written register here. A soft copy of the following documents in the prescribed form must be atttached with the application form before the last date of reciept of application form.(The last date of form recipt can be seen on the footer)

    1. Training Letter provided by College/Institute.
    2. Copy of marksheet of 10+2
    3. Copy of Mark sheet of Last Semester(Students completed four semesters of B Tech courses will be given preference).

    The application must be submited, before the last date of application submission. Form shall become unavailable after the last date has passed.

    The applicants who are serious and willing to move out for training only need to apply avoiding unnnecessary exercise to be done by ONGC and in turn ruling out the chances of other serious candidates.

    The schedule for students training every year will be as follows:

    4. Summer Training Winter Training
    Applying date 1st Feburary 1st October
    Last date of application submition On or before 15th April On or before 31st October
    Finalization of List On or before 10th May On or before 15th November
    Training Commences 15th May to 30th June 20th November to 31st December
    Training Ends 15th July to 30th August 20th January to 28th Feburary
  4. Selection Criteria
    • First preference will be given to immediate wards of ONGC Employee serving/retired.
    • Preference would be given to students from colleges in vicinity of the Work centers, Universities where ONGC constituted chairs exists and reputed Institutions like IIT's, ISM, RGIPT.
    • No preference would be given to friends and relatives of ONGC employees.
    • Summer/ winter training will be given only once to a student by ONGC.
  5. Completion Of Training
    1. On sucessful completion of the students training, the trainee will submit the following to the mentor:
      1. A soft copy report
      2. A hard copy report
    2. On the basis of the report, the Mentor will issue a letter of completion to the trainee specifying the starting of students training and the topic subject on which the trainng was conducted.
    3. No preference would be given to friends and relatives of ONGC employees.
    4. The completion letter along with a soft copy of the report and letter issued by the controlling authority for starting the students training to Training Coordinator of the concerned work center for issuing Certificate of completion
    5. Certificateof completeion issued by Coordinator training ONGCA on a standardiszed format of te certificate.

**The selected students will be e-mailed of their selection and may reply to ONGC academy of their selection.
